# VENDOR NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**Effective Date**: [DATE]

This Vendor Non-Disclosure Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into between:

**[COMPANY NAME]**, a [STATE/COUNTRY] [entity type], with offices at [ADDRESS] (the "**Company**"); and

**[VENDOR NAME]**, a [STATE/COUNTRY] [entity type], with offices at [ADDRESS] (the "**Vendor**").

## PURPOSE

Vendor will provide [SERVICES/PRODUCTS] to Company and may have access to Company's confidential information.

## 1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

**"Confidential Information"** means all information disclosed by Company to Vendor, including:

- Technical data, software, source code, designs, and specifications
- Business information, customer lists, pricing, and strategies
- Financial data and projections
- Product roadmaps and development plans
- Any information marked as confidential or that should reasonably be understood as confidential

## 2. VENDOR OBLIGATIONS

Vendor agrees to:

- (a) Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence
- (b) Use Confidential Information solely for providing services to Company
- (c) Not disclose to third parties without Company's written consent
- (d) Limit access to employees and subcontractors with a need to know
- (e) Bind all employees and subcontractors to equivalent confidentiality obligations
- (f) Implement reasonable security measures to protect Confidential Information
- (g) Notify Company promptly of any unauthorized disclosure or use

## 3. EXCLUSIONS

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

- (a) Is publicly available through no breach by Vendor
- (b) Was known to Vendor prior to disclosure
- (c) Is independently developed by Vendor
- (d) Is rightfully received from a third party
- (e) Must be disclosed by law (with prompt notice to Company)

## 4. DATA PROTECTION

If Confidential Information includes personal data:

- (a) Vendor shall comply with all applicable data protection laws
- (b) Vendor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
- (c) Vendor shall only process personal data as instructed by Company
- (d) Vendor shall assist Company with data subject requests and security incidents

## 5. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon project completion or Company's request, Vendor shall:

- (a) Return or destroy all Confidential Information
- (b) Provide written certification of destruction if requested
- (c) Ensure all subcontractors comply with these requirements

## 6. TERM

This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues for **[TERM, e.g., three (3) years]**. Confidentiality obligations survive for **[SURVIVAL, e.g., five (5) years]** after disclosure.

## 7. OWNERSHIP

All Confidential Information remains Company's property. No license or rights are granted except as necessary to perform services.

## 8. WARRANTY

Confidential Information is provided "AS IS" without warranty.

## 9. REMEDIES AND INDEMNIFICATION

9.1 Vendor acknowledges that breach will cause irreparable harm. Company is entitled to injunctive relief and other remedies.

9.2 Vendor shall indemnify and hold Company harmless from any losses arising from Vendor's breach of this Agreement.

## 10. GENERAL

**Amendment**: Only by written instrument signed by both parties.

**Assignment**: Vendor may not assign without Company's written consent.
