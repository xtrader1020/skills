# EMPLOYEE NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Employee Non-Disclosure Agreement (this "**Agreement**") is entered into between:

**[COMPANY NAME]**, a [STATE/COUNTRY] [entity type], with offices at [ADDRESS] (the "**Company**"); and

**[EMPLOYEE NAME]**, an individual residing at [ADDRESS] (the "**Employee**").

**Effective Date**: [DATE]

## 1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

During Employee's employment with the Company, Employee will have access to and become familiar with confidential and proprietary information (the "**Confidential Information**"), including but not limited to:

- Trade secrets, inventions, ideas, processes, formulas, data, and know-how
- Customer lists and information, pricing, marketing plans, and business strategies
- Financial information, including costs, profits, and sales data
- Product development plans, designs, specifications, and source code
- Personnel information and compensation structures
- Any information marked or identified as confidential

## 2. OBLIGATIONS

Employee agrees to:

- (a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
- (b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party during or after employment
- (c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Company's benefit
- (d) Not use Confidential Information for personal gain or competitive purposes
- (e) Take reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized disclosure
- (f) Not remove Confidential Information from Company premises without authorization

## 3. EXCLUSIONS

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

- (a) Is or becomes public knowledge through no fault of Employee
- (b) Was known to Employee prior to employment
- (c) Is independently developed by Employee after employment ends using no Confidential Information
- (d) Must be disclosed by law (with prompt notice to Company)

## 4. COMPANY PROPERTY

All documents, devices, and materials containing Confidential Information are Company property and must be returned upon termination of employment or upon request.

## 5. POST-EMPLOYMENT

Employee's obligations continue after employment ends for:
- **Trade secrets**: Indefinitely, as long as they remain trade secrets
- **Other Confidential Information**: [TERM, e.g., five (5) years] after employment ends

## 6. NO CONFLICTING OBLIGATIONS

Employee represents that this Agreement does not conflict with any prior agreement or obligation.

## 7. REMEDIES

Employee acknowledges that breach will cause irreparable harm. The Company is entitled to injunctive relief and any other available remedies.

## 8. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

**Important Notice**: This Agreement does not prohibit Employee from:

- (a) Reporting possible violations of law to government agencies
- (b) Participating in government investigations
- (c) Discussing wages, hours, or working conditions with co-workers (as protected under the National Labor Relations Act)
- (d) Filing a charge with or communicating with the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission, National Labor Relations Board, or similar agencies

Employee does not need Company approval or notice to make such protected disclosures.

**Defend Trade Secrets Act Notice**: Under 18 U.S.C. ยง 1833(b), Employee will not be held criminally or civilly liable under federal or state trade secret law for disclosure of a trade secret that is made:

- (a) In confidence to a government official or attorney solely for the purpose of reporting or investigating a suspected violation of law; or
- (b) In a complaint or other document filed in a lawsuit if made under seal.

## 9. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE/COUNTRY].
